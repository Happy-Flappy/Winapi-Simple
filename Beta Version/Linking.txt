Core Winsimple Linking:  -lgdi32 -luser32 -lgdiplus -lole32

Audio Linking: -lwinmm

Controls Linking: -lcomctl32 -lcomdlg32 -luuid -lshell32

Clipboard And DragNDrop Linking: -lole32 -luuid


Total Linking: -lgdi32 -luser32 -lgdiplus -lole32 -lwinmm -lcomctl32 -lshell32 -luuid

Note: You must always provide linking for the base of winsimple in order to use the other headers. Audio is an exception to that rule.
